Welcome and thanks for checking out my work! Below are projects I've worked on and a short blurb about them. I've tried to record any lessons I learned from each project, and you can see those in the More Details section of each.

Games

DIY or Die


The first game jam project I worked on. As part of Global Game Jam 2020's theme of repair, I joined a team to make a click and drag game about fixing your car while you're driving it through the desert.

Dangan Dodge


A 1v1 bullet hell game taking inspirations from fighting games. Basically the project where I got the most of my Unity experience from. Hoping to get it in a releasable state at some point.

Kim Nen


My first game! An auto biographical visual novel documenting my last year of college. Made with Ren'py, and I did everything from the "art" to the music and sound effects.

Dodge Hell


Predecessor to Dangan Dodge made together with some college friends. Playable web version featuring art direction from Jamuscles kept for posterity.

CU Bus

A bus app for the Champaign-Urbana area. Utilizes the CU MTD API and the Google Maps API to present bus data in a clean and concise presentation.
